Passenger Elevators

Passenger elevators are a type of elevator that can be designed as electric or hydraulic, used for passenger transport. Comfort, aesthetics and safety are always at the forefront in passenger elevators.

Freight Elevators

Freight elevators are a type of elevator that can be designed as electric or hydraulic, used for the purpose of carrying goods and people. The priority in freight elevators is durability, safety and high carrying capacity.

Dumbwaiter

Dumbwaiters are used to transport service materials such as plates, glasses and food from the kitchen to the dining floor in hotels, social facilities, schools and villas. Electric types are available.

Stretcher Lifts

Stretcher lifts are used to transport patients in healthcare institutions such as hospitals and clinics. It should have sufficient cabin space, as it is necessary to ride on the stretcher with the patient and the attendants next to him.

Periodic Maintenance

No matter how good the elevators are, if their maintenance is not done on time and well, they cannot have a safe, efficient and continuous healthy operation. Although elevator maintenance is a legal obligation, it is obligatory and important in terms of life safety, time, comfort and the service life of the elevator. Periodic maintenance of elevators; It ensures that they operate safely all the time, the failure rate decreases, accidents are prevented by pre-detection of materials that may fail due to natural use, thanks to the regular control of expensive elevator components such as elevator machine, control panel, the life of these devices is extended and they can work for a long time without malfunctioning at lower costs. In addition to all these, it is a legal obligation to have elevator maintenance. Building managers should have elevator maintenance done by elevator companies that have the authorization certificates specified in the elevator regulation. Otherwise, building managers are responsible for all situations that may occur in the elevator. You can contact us for more detailed information.

Lift Modernization

Lift modernization is the general name of the process for the improvement of an existing elevator. Elevators used in buildings may need revision for many reasons. This will be the renewal of the parts of the existing elevator that have completed its life, as well as the need for an elevator revision process as a result of legal label inspections. An actively operating elevator needs to go through a series of revision processes in order to bring it to the safety and comfort level specified in the latest elevator regulation and to ensure that your elevator has a green label that registers that it is safe. It is therefore necessary to renovate the facility in order to maintain the quality of all components used and to prevent the buildings from depreciating. Thanks to the elevator revision; A safer use is ensured by increasing safety standards, and a more aesthetic appearance is created by the introduction of new products. More comfortable operation of the elevator is ensured, and high energy savings are achieved.
